
# Agile Role Reflection

## My Role

I work as a **mobile app development intern** at Focus Bear. My role involves building and improving app features, fixing bugs, and ensuring a smooth user experience. I contribute to the Agile process by delivering incremental updates, participating in sprint planning, and adapting based on team feedback.

---

## Contribution to the Agile Process

* **Iteration:** I develop features in small, manageable parts that can be reviewed and improved quickly.
* **Collaboration:** I communicate progress and blockers regularly during team discussions.
* **Responsiveness:** I adjust priorities based on feedback from testers, designers, and supervisors.
* **Quality assurance:** I help ensure each sprint delivers functional, tested components.

---

## Interaction with Other Roles

* **Designers:** Collaborate to translate UI/UX designs into functional screens.
* **Backend Developers:** Coordinate data structures and API integrations.
* **Testers/QA:** Respond to reported issues and ensure bug fixes meet acceptance criteria.
* **Supervisors:** Provide progress updates, clarify requirements, and align tasks with sprint goals.
