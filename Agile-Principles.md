
# Agile Reflection

## Benefits of Agile

* **Flexibility:** Agile allows quick adaptation to feedback and changing requirements.
* **Continuous improvement:** Regular retrospectives help identify and fix workflow issues.
* **Collaboration:** Encourages open communication between developers, designers, and stakeholders.
* **Faster delivery:** Work is released in small, functional increments, ensuring early value.

---

## Challenges of Agile

* **Scope creep:** Constant changes can lead to unclear or shifting goals if not managed properly.
* **Coordination issues:** Requires consistent communication; remote teams may find this difficult.
* **Incomplete documentation:** Emphasis on speed can sometimes lead to missing details.
* **Dependence on discipline:** Teams must stay engaged and organized to keep sprints effective.

---

## Applying Agile Principles to My Role

* **Iterative development:** Break down features into smaller deliverables for quicker feedback.
* **Daily communication:** Stay transparent about progress and blockers to keep collaboration smooth.
* **Continuous learning:** Reflect after each sprint to improve code quality and workflow.
* **User focus:** Prioritize user experience by regularly testing and refining based on feedback.
